The History and Processing of Pearson Hot Sauce

2017 - Huge Crop and Success

Red Savinas did NOT grow. The chocolate habanero and Carolina Reaper crossed. (Cocoa Reaper).
Plants started by my son in February, practically became trees by summer end. 7ft tall! Large variety and also added guajillo.
Planned to have Reapers as a separate process, but in the end, just blended it all together.
Had to get bigger buckets and a 6gal carboy. Ordered a whiskey barrel. 1 gal in brown bottle with oak planks. 6 gallons to distribute.

2016 - First Year Success!

First batch over flowed.
Good flavor is primary goal. Good heat that is not painful is second goal.
Primary was Red Savina and some pepper that was packaged as Carolina Reaper, but was not. Roasted many ingredients for flavor.
Basically a gallon poured into 5oz bottles for friends and family.

How It Started

Enter the desire to make hot sauce that I liked. Rich flavor and the fun of making it. Give credit to fermentation process.
